 Alejandro Laguna - Biografia
[português]

Music
Tango Dance

Composition studies in La Plata University and musical education in
"Manuel de Falla" Music Municipal Conservatory of Buenos Aires. Here he got his musical guitar studies with some international well known concertists as Eduardo Egues, Eduardo Isaac and Miguel Angel Girollet. Singing studies with Susana Naidich (phonoaudiologist and singer in Colon Theatre in Buenos Aires)

He formed and directed "Tormenta Tropica" the first Portuguese salsa
orchestra, with Latin American musicians.
This orchestra has played successfully and during three years gave concerts in Spain, Italy and Portugal in Centro Cultural de Belém, 2nd SIC anniversary and Lisbon Parties.

He composed music recorded in Music Sony such as "Ay Maria" and Danza Café, television programme generic.

He works as a musical attendant in Lisbon Superior School of Dance.

He gives conferences about "Tango and Fado" in Latin American House.
