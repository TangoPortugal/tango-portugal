Through Milonga d'A Barraca, Alejandro promoted encounters between Tango and Fado for over two decades. Approximately 80 fado singers performed at Milonga d'A Barraca, including Celeste Rodrigues, António Chainho, Katia Guerreiro, among many others.

In 2024, Camila and Alejandro present their interpretation, called "A Dance Embrace in Fado," at Got Talent Portugal (semifinalists).
