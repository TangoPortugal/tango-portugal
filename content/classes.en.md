### Performances

**Tango Dance** (Tango, Vals and Milonga)\
"Um Abraço Dançado em Fado" exhibitions. For this presentation Fado Live is available too.

**FADO DANCE** is a practice created by Alejandro in 1999 in the Madragoa neighborhood and developed for over 25 years. Since 2023, Alejandro has begun to dance and develop the relationship between Fado and Tango.
